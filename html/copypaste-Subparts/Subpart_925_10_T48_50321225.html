Subpart 925.10 - Additional Foreign Acquisition Regulations

Parent topic: PART 925 - FOREIGN ACQUISITION

925.1001 Waiver of right to examination of records.

(b) Determination and findings. A determination and findings required by 48 CFR 25.1001(b) shall be forwarded to either the Director, Office of Contract Management, Office of Procurement and Assistance Management, or for the National Nuclear Security Administration (NNSA), to the Deputy Associate Administrator for Acquisition and Project Management, for coordination of the Secretary's approval.
